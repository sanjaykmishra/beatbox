Mike Acme on what's next for Acme Corp

By Profile Reporter · January 14, 2026

Acme Corp CEO Mike Acme — yes, the family name is also the company name — sat down with us to talk about the second half of the year. The company, which raised $30 million in Series B funding last fall, has been quietly retooling its enterprise sales motion after a slower-than-expected mid-market quarter.

Acme (Mike, not the company) said the team is doubling down on workflow automation in financial services. "We tried to be everything to everyone, and that was a mistake," he said. "Now we're focused, and the pipeline reflects that."

Acme Corp employs around 60 people in San Francisco and a small New York office that opened in March. The company plans to be cash-flow positive by Q4 2026.

Asked about competition from larger workflow players, Mike Acme demurred: "We're a different shape. We don't try to be a platform. We solve a single, painful workflow exceptionally well, and we charge for it."

The company expects to announce a major financial-services partnership in February.
