Portal URL
portal.beat.app
One portal URL across all your clients — users sign in with email and only see reports for their own client.
Authentication
Magic link via email. No passwords. Links expire in 30 minutes. Sessions last 14 days then re-auth.
What portal users can see
✓
Reports for this client only, where "Publish to portal" is on
✓
Branded with your agency identity, never Beat's
✕
No access to drafts, pitches, journalist data, or other clients
✕
No edit affordances anywhere — portal is strictly read-only
✕
No internal team activity or audit log
Security note
The portal is a separate trust domain — portal sessions cannot access internal endpoints, and internal sessions cannot access portal endpoints. Every portal access is logged in workspace activity.
